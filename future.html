Future Aspirations

My career goals, dreams, and vision for the future

My Vision for the Future

As I progress through my Software Engineer degree at Kirinyaga University, I'm constantly thinking about how I can apply my skills and knowledge to make a meaningful impact. My aspirations extend beyond just getting a job - I want to contribute to technological advancement in Kenya and Africa as a whole.

Career Goals

01

Short-term Goals (1-3 years)

  • Complete my Software Engineer degree with First Class Honors
  • Secure an internship at a leading tech company in Kenya
  • Develop a portfolio of at least 5 substantial software projects
  • Contribute to open-source projects relevant to African tech
  • Obtain AWS or Google Cloud certification
02

Medium-term Goals (3-5 years)

  • Work as a software engineer at a reputable tech company
  • Specialize in backend development or machine learning
  • Mentor other aspiring developers from similar backgrounds
  • Start a tech blog sharing knowledge and experiences
  • Develop a tech solution addressing a local community challenge
03

Long-term Goals (5-10 years)

  • Found a tech startup focused on solving African problems
  • Establish a tech incubator in Eldoret to nurture local talent
  • Become a thought leader in African tech innovation
  • Invest in and mentor other African tech entrepreneurs
  • Contribute to making Kenya a global tech hub

Dream Projects

AgriTech Solution for Small Farmers

Develop a mobile platform connecting small-scale farmers in Kenya with markets, providing weather information, and offering agricultural advice via AI.

Telemedicine Platform for Rural Areas

Create an accessible telemedicine solution to connect patients in remote areas with doctors, particularly focusing on maternal and child health.

EdTech Platform for Rural Schools

Build an educational technology platform that provides quality learning resources to schools in underserved communities across Kenya.

Personal Development Vision

Continuous Learning

I believe in lifelong learning. Beyond my formal education, I plan to continuously update my skills through online courses, certifications, and staying current with technological advancements.

Work-Life Balance

While pursuing ambitious career goals, I also value maintaining a healthy work-life balance, nurturing personal relationships, and contributing to my community.

Giving Back

I aspire to establish scholarship programs for bright students from my hometown of Eldoret who want to pursue technology careers but lack financial resources.

Giving Back to Community

The future belongs to those who believe in the beauty of their dreams. As a Kenyan tech enthusiast, I dream of a future where African solutions solve African problems, and where technology becomes a true equalizer in our society.

- My Personal Mantra
